Introducing NETO

Neto Gamer company has introduced NETO token on the Polygon platform Contract Address: 0xed0c8e710495d45b462338751868f2ed79295a6c exclusively designed for its gaming platforms, allowing users to benefit from various features and capabilities within the game by acquiring tokens. An important point to note is that this company uses a unique token for all its games, which sets it apart from its competitors. Now, individuals who register on the platform for this company's income-generating activities will receive a certain amount of tokens as a gift when activating each package. This enables them to enhance their gaming experience right from the start

Introducing NETO

The liquidity pool of Neto Token is based on Matic, and it does not have the possibility of price reduction. Token holders do not have the ability to withdraw liquidity or mint tokens without depositing Matic. This token has permanently locked 100 Matic for the initial pricing, generating 1,000,000 Neto in exchange. This Neto amount is also permanently frozen. The initial price of Neto is set at 0.0001 Matic based on the deposited amounts, and with each transaction, its price increases

Token Purchase and Sale Fees

Users buying the token will incur a 2% fee based on Matic, with all of that fee being deposited into the liquidity pool. Similarly, a 2% fee based on Matic will be charged for selling the token, with all of that fee being added to the liquidity pool. These Matic deposits into the liquidity pool lead to an increase in the price of the Neto token, resulting in profits for token holders

Token Transfer Fees

When a user transfers their tokens to another user's account, no new tokens will be minted. The sending user will pay a 2% fee in Neto, and the receiving user will pay a 2% fee in Neto. Out of these fees, 2% of the sender's tokens and 2% of the receiver's tokens will be burned. With the constant Matic liquidity and a decreasing number of Neto tokens due to burning, the Neto token's price will increase, leading to profits for its holders

Development Team's Online Games

In exchange for the received fees, the development team will design online games on the blockchain platform. These games, being designed as smart contracts, offer traceability and accurate invoicing for their earnings. 20% of the fees collected from these games will be directly and automatically deposited into the liquidity pool through the blockchain. With the liquidity pool's volume increasing and the number of Neto tokens remaining constant, the price of Neto will rise, resulting in profits for token holders

NFT Marketplace

All games in the NetoGamer collection have purchasable NFTs. Users need to use the Neto token for buying and selling these NFTs. Transactions in this section will lead to an increase in transaction fees paid, consequently contributing to the increase in the Neto token price
